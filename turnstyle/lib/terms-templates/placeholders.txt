Here are the {{VALUE}} placeholders the template wizard resolves (from AUTO_VARS in the terms wizard):

Variable	Source	Description
{{PROMOTER_NAME}}	Campaign → promoter name	Promoter’s display name
{{PROMOTER_ABN}}	Campaign → promoter ABN	Promoter’s ABN
{{PROMOTER_ADDRESS}}	Campaign → promoter address	Formatted address (street, suburb, state, postcode)
{{PROMO_START}}	Campaign → promo start date	Promo start, long format (e.g. “Monday, 1 January 2024”)
{{PROMO_END}}	Campaign → promo end date	Promo end, long format
{{DRAW_MECHANIC}}	Campaign → mechanic type	e.g. “Trade Lottery”, “Limited Offer”, “Prize Draw”
{{CAMPAIGN_URL}}	Derived	https://turnstylehost.com/campaign/{tsCode}/
{{REGION}}	Campaign → regions	e.g. “throughout Australia” or “in NSW, VIC, QLD”
{{ENTRY_MECHANIC}}	Campaign → entry mechanic	How to enter (e.g. “online form”, “trade account”)
{{TOTAL_WINNERS}}	Campaign → prizes	Total number of winners (sum of prize quantities)
{{DRAW_DATE}}	Derived from promo end	5th business day after promo end
{{PRIZE_LIST}}	Campaign → prizes	Formatted list (qty × description, value)
{{PRIZE_POOL}}	Campaign → prizes	Total prize pool, formatted (e.g. “$10,000.00”)
{{UNCLAIMED_DEADLINE}}	Wizard answer (or derived)	60 days after promo end (e.g. “1 March 2024”)
{{UNCLAIMED_REDRAW}}	Wizard answer (or derived)	Day after unclaimed deadline (redraw date)
Notes

{{UNCLAIMED_DEADLINE}} and {{UNCLAIMED_REDRAW}} are pre-filled from promo end but can be edited in the wizard (gap answers).
Any {{KEY}} not in this list is left as [KEY] in the output.
[[key]] (double brackets) are gap placeholders filled by wizard questions (e.g. [[NOTIFICATION_METHOD]], [[ADDITIONAL_ENTRIES]]), not from this auto-vars list.
